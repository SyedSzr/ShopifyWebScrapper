Ultimate Shopify Scraper

Ultimate Shopify Scraper is a product research and catalog-prep platform for Shopify workflows. It scrapes products from one or multiple stores, enriches them with SEO-ready content, provides actionable catalog analytics, and lets you export data in JSON and Shopify CSV formats for fast publishing.

Complete Description

This site is built for store owners, drop-shipping operators, and agencies who need a faster way to collect products, standardize content, and prepare listings for launch. You can scrape large catalogs, filter by AI category, inspect product details, generate SEO titles and descriptions with your own brand identity, and export product data in one workflow. Multi-store mode helps you merge catalog data across multiple source stores, while bulk automation runs scrape + SEO in one click.

All Features

Single-Store Scraping

Scrape products page-by-page from a Shopify store URL and preview results instantly in the dashboard.

Multi-Store Support

Add multiple source URLs and scrape all stores in one run. Products are merged into one working catalog.

Bulk Automation

One-click pipeline that runs multi-store scraping and automatic SEO generation for all collected products.

AI Category Mapping

Auto-classifies products into practical categories to speed up filtering and Shopify-ready organization.

SEO Rewrite Engine

Generates SEO titles/descriptions with your store branding and replaces source-store mentions with your name.

Product Analytics Dashboard

Shows total products, category count, average/highest/lowest price, and most-used tags for fast catalog review.

AI Trend Analysis

Highlights top categories, keyword trends, average pricing, and premium niches from the current dataset.

Flexible Exports

Export full catalog or individual products as JSON and Shopify CSV with category-based file naming.

Detail Product Viewer

Open a popup for each product with description, variants, images, and SEO fields for quick QA checks.

How It Works

Step 1: Add one store URL or a list of multiple store URLs.
Step 2: Run scraping or bulk automation to collect products.
Step 3: Apply SEO generation using your store identity.
Step 4: Review analytics and trend insights.
Step 5: Export full or individual products to JSON/CSV.
